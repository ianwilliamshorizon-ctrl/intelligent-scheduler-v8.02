# Standard Operating Procedure: Customer and Vehicle Management

**Objective:** To ensure all customer and vehicle information is accurately recorded and maintained within the system. This SOP covers the creation, editing, and viewing of customer and vehicle profiles.

---

## 1. Customer Management

Accurate customer information is vital for communication, billing, and service history.

### Creating a New Customer

1.  Navigate to the **Customers** section from the main menu.
2.  Click the **"Add New Customer"** button.
3.  A form will appear. Fill in the customer's details:
    *   **Personal Information:** Forename, Surname, Phone, Email.
    *   **Company Information (if applicable):** Company Name.
    *   **Address:** Address Line 1, City, Postcode.
4.  Click **"Save"** to create the new customer profile.

### Viewing and Editing a Customer

1.  From the **Customers** list, you can search for a customer by name, company, or phone number.
2.  Click on a customer in the list to open their detailed view.
3.  The detailed view shows:
    *   Customer contact information.
    *   A list of their associated vehicles.
    *   Their job, estimate, and invoice history.
4.  To edit the customer's details, click the **"Edit"** button on their profile.
5.  Make the necessary changes and click **"Save"**. 

---

## 2. Vehicle Management

Each job is linked to a vehicle, so maintaining an accurate vehicle database is essential.

### Creating a New Vehicle

*   **From the Vehicle Section:**
    1.  Navigate to the **Vehicles** section from the main menu.
    2.  Click the **"Add New Vehicle"** button.
    3.  You must first select the **Customer** who owns the vehicle.
    4.  Enter the vehicle's details:
        *   **Registration:** The vehicle's license plate.
        *   **Make and Model.**
        *   **VIN (Vehicle Identification Number).**
        *   Other details like engine size, fuel type, and color.
    5.  Click **"Save"**.

*   **When Creating an Estimate or Job:**
    1.  If you are creating a new estimate or job for a vehicle not yet in the system, you can add it directly from the estimate/job creation form.
    2.  Next to the vehicle selection dropdown, click the **"Add New Vehicle"** button.
    3.  A simplified form will appear. If the customer is already selected, they will be pre-filled.
    4.  Enter the vehicle registration and other key details.
    5.  Click **"Save"**. The new vehicle will be created and automatically linked to the estimate or job.

### Viewing and Editing a Vehicle

1.  From the **Vehicles** list, search for a vehicle by its registration, make, or model.
2.  Click on a vehicle to open its detailed view.
3.  This view shows:
    *   Full vehicle details.
    *   The owner's name (with a link to their profile).
    *   A complete service history (jobs, estimates, invoices).
4.  Click the **"Edit"** button to update the vehicle's information. 

---

**Best Practices:**

*   **Check for Duplicates:** Before creating a new customer or vehicle, always perform a quick search to ensure they don't already exist in the system.
*   **Registration is Key:** The vehicle registration is the primary identifier. Ensure it is entered correctly.
*   **Link Vehicles to Customers:** Always ensure a vehicle is linked to the correct customer owner.
